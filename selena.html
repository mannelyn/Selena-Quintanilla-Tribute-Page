Selena Quintanilla Pérez
1971 - 1995
Portrait of Selena Quintanilla Pérez

Here is a timeline of Selena
Quintanilla's life:

1971

• On April 16th, Selena Quintanilla was born in Lake Jackson, Texas

1975

• Selana’s father forms the band “Selena y Los Dinos”

1980

• Selena makes her musical debut in her father’s restaurant,
Papa Gayos

1986

• Selena y Los Dinos’ first record is released ‘Muñequito Ee Trapo’

1987

• Won for the first time Female Vocalist of the Year at the
Tejano Music Awards

1988

• Selena met a guitarist named Chris Perez

1989

• The Quintanilla family hires Chris Perez to be in the band

• Signed by EMI Latin

1990

•Her first EMI-Latin album, “Selena” is released

1991

• First female Tejano recording artist to score a Gold Record with
“Ven Conmigo”

• Selena has her first #1 song, “Buenos Amigos” a duet with
Alvaro Torres

1992

• Selena marries Chris Perez on April 2nd

1993

• Selena Etc. clothing manufacturing business is launched

• Selena holds her first Mexican press conference in Monterrey

• Selena appears in the Mexican Soap Opera “Dos mujeres, un camino”

1994

• Selena receives a Double Platinum award for “Selena Live”

• Selena receives a Quadruple Platinum award for “Amor Prohibido”

• Grammy for best Mexican American performance for “Selena Live”

• Won a role as singer in the film “Don Juan DeMarco”

• Band debuted in New York, Los Angeles, Argentina & Puerto Rico

• Becomes the first female crossover Latin artist signed to SBK, sister label to EMI Latin

1995

• Was in process of recording her first English language album that was expected to launch her into a crossover career

• Won six Tejano Music Awards, including top female vocalist for
the eighth time

• Nominated for a second Grammy for “Amor Prohibido”

• Selena performs before a record-breaking crowd of more than 61,000 fans at the Houston Astrodome (February 26th)

• Selena is murdered by her associate Yolanda Saldivar on
Friday, March 31st
